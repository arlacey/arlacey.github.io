The Majestic

Travelling to North Adams, Massachusetts, for me, was probably the most inspiring and pleasant surprises of my journey. Despite being one of the smallest towns in Massachusetts, the colorful murals painted on almost every street corner bring the whole place to life. The town being surrounded by majestic mountains only enhances the whimsical, godly, dreamlike vibe that I love about this town.
