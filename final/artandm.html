Discovery

Going on field trips with my class allowed me to both experience and really understand how diverse the world of art really is. Doing so also inspired me to take every opportunity to travel to new places. Places featured in this segment include: Mass MoCA (Massachusetts), Storm King (New York), The Whitney (NY), The MET (NY), and The Guggenheim (NY).
