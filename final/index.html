Welcome!

Within this collage of images you shall experience a photographic journey (all photos taken by me) of places I’ve explored and sojourned over a span of 3 years in college. From wandering a range of surprisingly innovative art museums, striking urban architecture, and serene landscapes, I’ve come to realize just how immensely these visits and explorations have both enhanced my college experience and overall approach to experiencing life.

While viewing this gallery of pure imagery, my intent is to convey and share with you the wonder, awe and quietude that I’ve experienced, so far, in hopes of inspiring you to embark on a journey of your own.
